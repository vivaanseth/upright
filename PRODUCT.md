# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who spend long stretches at a desk and want a private, low-friction reminder to reset their posture. They use Posture while working, studying, designing, or gaming and need it to recede into the background after a brief calibration.

## Product Purpose

Posture turns an ordinary webcam into a local-only posture signal. It compares the user's current upper-body landmarks with a personal baseline, gives calm feedback after sustained slouching, and summarizes the current session without uploading or retaining camera imagery.

Success means the app is trustworthy enough to leave running, clear enough to calibrate without instruction, quiet enough not to interrupt concentration, and reliable across macOS, Windows, and mainstream Linux desktops.

## Brand Personality

Calm, observant, and candid. The app should feel quietly capable rather than clinical, gamified, or judgmental. Copy is short, direct, and supportive.

## Anti-references

- Medical dashboards that imply diagnosis or treatment.
- Gamified wellness products built around streak anxiety, badges, or guilt.
- Neon cyberpunk camera tools and generic purple AI gradients.
- Beige lifestyle-wellness branding and decorative glass cards.
- Dense analytics screens that make a simple posture check feel like work.

## Design Principles

1. Make privacy visible. Explain local processing before asking for access and never hide camera state.
2. Show one decision at a time. Onboarding, calibration, and recovery each have a single primary action.
3. Use calm feedback. Reserve strong color and motion for meaningful state changes, never punishment.
4. Earn the background. The tray, nudge, and session behavior must be predictable enough to trust all day.
5. Be honest about uncertainty. Poor landmark confidence is Unknown, never Poor.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. All states use text and iconography in addition to color. Every workflow is keyboard accessible, focus is always visible, zoom to 200% remains usable, and reduced-motion and system theme preferences are respected. Language avoids medical claims, body judgment, and blame.
