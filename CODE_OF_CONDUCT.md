# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of positive behavior include empathy, respectful feedback, accountability, and attention to accessibility and privacy. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, sexualized language, personal attacks, publishing private information, and body-shaming.

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that conflict with these standards. Reports should use the repository's private security or maintainer contact channels once published.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
